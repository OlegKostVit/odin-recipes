Bikini Sandwiches

Description

Bikini sandwiches consist of buttered toasted bread topped with Manchego cheese and serrano ham. Each bite is warm, buttery, and layered with subtle tang and umami.

Ingredients

  • 4 (1/2 inch thick) slices heaty white bread, such as Farmhouse
  • 2 ounces thinly sliced serrano ham
  • 4 ounces Manchego cheese, shredded
  • 2 tablespoons salted butter, softened

Steps

  1. Top 2 bread slices evenly with serrano ham, followed by a sprinkle of 1/2 cup Manchego on each. Place remaining 2 bread slices on top of Manchego. Spread butter evenly on both sides of sandwiches.
  2. Heat a large nonstick skillet over medium. Add sandwiches to skillet; lightly cover with aluminum foil, then place a large cast-iron skillet on top to compress. (Alternatively, use a heavy skillet weighted with 2 heavy cans.) Cook, flipping sandwiches once and adjusting heat as necessary, until bread is golden brown and toasted and cheese melts, 2 to 3 minutes per side.
  3. Transfer sandwiches to a cutting board. Cut the crusts off, then cut into triangles. Serve immediately.
